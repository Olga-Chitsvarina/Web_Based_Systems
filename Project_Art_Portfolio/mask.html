"TOGETHERNESS IN ISOLATION"

SUMMARY:

When I was working on this project, I thought about people who live in isolation, keep social and physical distances because of COVID-19. Some of them are at home with relatives, others are in hospitals and can communicate with their family only virtually.



Self-isolating people are often wearing masks to protect themselves and others from COVID-19 illness. Masks hide ½ of the face and people cannot read each other’s facial expressions effectively, that makes communication difficult. In order to help people feel Hygge, even if they keep social distancing and wear masks, I designed a mask with these features:



Light:



Light around the mask creates a warm atmosphere. Imagine patients staying in a hospital room during a holiday. Candles are not usually allowed in hospitals, but almost everyone wears masks. They can turn lights on the mask to help everyone feel the Hygge atmosphere.



Fan:



Many people associate Hygge with drinking delicious beverages. Imagine someone wearing a mask, drinking hot beverages. They either have to remove the mask (which is not safe) or use a straw to drink it. Fan kan be kept in a pocket until needed. Fan can be directed to a cup with a beverage to make it cooler so that they can still drink a hot beverage but does not burn taste buds.



Music:



Individuals staying inside during self-isolation may miss sounds of the outside world. With the mask, they can listen to birds chirping and imagine themselves being outside.



Screen:



People can express their feelings and emotions better with my mask. They can select a picture that they want others to see on their face using their phone. Or can turn speech recognition feature so that pictures are updated based on words used in sentence/context.
